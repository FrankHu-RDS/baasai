5 ways BaaS Marketing can grow your customer base

Whether your selling direct to consumer or other businesses we have marketing tools which can help you market and grow your customer base.

“In the world of Internet Customer Service, it’s important to remember your competitor is only one mouse click away."

– Doug Warner

1.  SEO Optimization

The latest consumer research shows that 90% thats right 9-0 percent of consumers find the businesses they work with on the first or second page of Google Search results. That is why every BaaS package starts with a website that is SEO optimized to help bring you to the top of the Google search results. Your website will help create a unique channel to let you engage with customers unlike many social media platforms or industry apps like DoorDash, UberEats etc. We also execute a cohesive SEO strategy where we look at social media backlinking strategies, content analysis, and many other factors to ensure you are optimized as much as possible for Google Search.

2. Automated Email Marketing

A crucial component to any marketing campaign includes marketing emails. We can help schedule and send emails to your customer base. Email can be a effective tool to alert customers to new products, services, promotions and limited time offers. This simple strategy can be highly successful marketing channel when executed properly. We can tailor a automated email strategy as part of your BaaS Marketing suite to engage with your customers proactively and help generate repeat business.

3. Website Blogging

We help equip your website with blogging capabilities so you can communicate with your customer base in new ways. Whether its blogging about your latest product or service, sharing tips and tricks or creating educational content for your audience there is endless ways blogging can help you grow your audience and customer base. 

4. LinkedIn Marketing

Are you a B2B business and looking for new ways to market and promote your business? We have automated LinkedIn Marketing tools to help grow your network, post content and gain potential business leads. LinkedIn is a underrated social platform which can provide your sales team direct access to key decision makers in your target businesses. If your company isn’t fully leveraging the power of LinkedIn then your business could be missing out on a huge number of potential business opportunities.

5. Website Analytics

One of the most common mistakes small business owners make is not leveraging their website analytics to help inform their other marketing efforts and learn about their target audience. Site builders like Wix don’t provide in-depth insights and analytics or the know how to fully leverage those details for business owners. Traditional website developers may not even provide their customers the information at all. Every month we provide customers with critical information like website heat maps, page traffic, where your website visitors come from and much more to help inform where to spend your marketing dollars effectively. Let us show you what the power of your website data can do to help grow your business.
