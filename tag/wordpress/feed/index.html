This blog is to explain how to update your schedule with your appointment booking system from BaaS.

First you’ll need to make sure you have an account and password setup with your website, this should have been provided at some point before. If you don’t have this information please reach out to us at baasaicovingtonla@gmail.com, and we’ll provide it to you.

You can update your schedule by going to the my account page below. You’ll need to replace <Your-Domain-Name> with your respective website’s name.
https://<Your-Domain-Name>.com/my-account

Then login, once you’ve logged in you should see a page with your account and some items at the top of the header. Click on the settings wrench button as seen in the picture below.


After clicking on the wrench that says settings at the top right after logging in.

You’ll see the items shown in the picture below. You will be able to set your services, schedule, breaks, and location in this page.

Update your hours for holiday times or special events by using the “Special Schedule” and “Days Off” sections seen below.

Click on the + button on the right to expand the section to utilize it!

Congratulations! You’ve updated your schedule hours to reflect properly! Now your clients will see your new hours when trying to book an appointment.
